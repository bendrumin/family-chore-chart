You Asked — We Built It: Per-Chore Reward Amounts

Set exactly how much each chore is worth

Hey ChoreStar families,

For a while now, one of the most common requests we've heard is: "Can we set different amounts for each chore?" Making the bed and scrubbing the bathroom shouldn't pay the same — and now they don't have to.

Today we're rolling out Per-Chore Rewards, a new way to handle allowance that gives each chore its own dollar value.

💰 How Per-Chore Rewards Work

Head to Settings → Family → Reward Settings and you'll find a new toggle:

Getting Set Up (takes 2 minutes)

🔀
Flip the toggle to "Per Chore" Switch from the flat daily rate to per-chore mode in one click
✏️
Set each chore's reward Open any chore and enter how much it's worth — $0.25, $1.00, $2.00, whatever fits your family
📊
Earnings add up automatically Each completed chore adds its amount to the weekly total — no math required
💵
Chore cards show the amount Each chore displays its reward right on the card so kids always know what they're earning
Example: Emma's Week
🛏️ Make Bed (×5 days) $2.50
🍽️ Clear Dishes (×5 days) $1.25
🐕 Walk the Dog (×3 days) $3.00
🧹 Vacuum Room (×1 day) $2.00
Emma's Weekly Total $8.75

✨ What's New

🔀
Flat Rate or Per Chore — Your Choice
The flat daily rate is still there if you prefer simplicity. Flip the toggle anytime to switch between modes. Both work great.
💰
Reward Amount on Every Chore Card
In Per Chore mode, each chore shows its dollar value right on the card. Kids know exactly what they're working toward.
📈
Automatic Weekly Totals
Weekly earnings calculate automatically based on what was actually completed. No spreadsheets, no guessing.

🎉 Full Week Bonus Reward

We also updated the weekly bonus. Instead of a dollar amount, you can now set a fun reward label — "pizza night", "movie night", "stay up late", anything your family loves.

🏆 How it works

When a child completes every chore every day of the week, a celebration toast pops up with your bonus label. "🎉 Perfect week! Bonus: pizza night!" — that kind of moment goes a long way toward building habits that actually stick.

Set your bonus label in Settings → Family → Full Week Bonus Reward.
